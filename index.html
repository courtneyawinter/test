Meet Courtney

Hello! I am an ambitious world traveler who has already seen amazing parts of the world at just the age of 23. From touring the cities of Spain to walking the golden beaches of Aruba, I love to immerse myself in different cultures. I am a small town girl who grew up in East Aurora, which is located near Buffalo New York, USA. After creating some awesome life memories while abroad, I figured it would be a good idea to keep track of my travels...so here we are! I cannot wait to continue my journey and share my love of exploring with you all.

Bon Voyage! -C
